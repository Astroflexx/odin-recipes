Chole Bhature

Description

Chole Bhature is a beloved North Indian dish featuring Chole (spiced chickpea curry) and Bhature (deep-fried bread). The chickpea curry is cooked in a flavorful tomato-based gravy with spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. It's garnished with cilantro, onions, and lemon juice. The Bhature, made from a dough of all-purpose flour and yogurt, is deep-fried to a crispy golden perfection. This dish is a popular street food and home-cooked meal, often served with pickles, onions, and yogurt to balance the flavors. Chole Bhature offers a delightful combination of spicy, tangy chickpeas and soft, fluffy, deep-fried bread, making it a cherished part of Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chickpea
  • Gravy
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Buy chickpeas
  2. Make the gravy
  3. Put chickpeas in gravy
  4. Add spices
  5. Add butter
  6. Stir well and cook on stove for 30 min
  7. Serve fresh and hot
